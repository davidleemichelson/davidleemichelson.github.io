David Michelson's Chicken Shawarma Dinner Recipe

===== Ingredients =====

Chicken
- 1 lb boneless skinless chicken breast
- 1 lb boneless skinless chicken thighs

Marinade
- 2 tablespoons olive oil
- 1.5 lemons-worth of juice
- 3/4 head of garlic, crushed
- 2 teaspoons cumin
- 2 teaspoons paprika
- 1.25 teaspoons Schwartz Mixed Spice (Ground Cinnamon (Cassia )(40%), Ground Coriander (38%), Caraway, Nutmeg (4%), Ginger, Cloves)
- 3/4 teaspoon turmeric
- 1 pinch garlic powder
- 1 pinch chili powder
- 1/2 teaspoon oregano
- salt and pepper, as much as you would normally put on the chicken at hand

Salad
- 1/2 head of parsley
- 3 salad tomatos
- 1 head of baby gem lettuce

Dressing
- some olive oil
- a little less white wine vineager
- 1/2 lemon, squeezed
- salt and pepper to taste

Sides
- as much hummus as you so please
- toum (lebanese garlic sauce, either buy it or google how to make it)
- pickled raddishes, onions, and banana peppers (or whatever pickled things you prefer)
- some rice, however much you want, I just used some lime-corriander kind that comes in a bag

===== Actually using the ingredients =====

I. THE CHICKEN
Step 1: Preparing the marinade. Get a bowl and add these ingredients:
- Mix:
- 4 tablespoons plain Greek yogurt
- 2 tablespoons olive oil
- 1.5 lemons-worth of juice
- 3/4 head of garlic, crushed
- 2 teaspoons cumin
- 2 teaspoons paprika
- 1.25 teaspoons Schwartz Mixed Spice (Ground Cinnamon (Cassia )(40%), Ground Coriander (38%), Caraway, Nutmeg (4%), Ginger, Cloves)
- 3/4 teaspoon turmeric
- 1 pinch garlic powder
- 1 pinch chili powder
- 1/2 teaspoon oregano
- salt and pepper, as much as you would normally put on the chicken at hand

Step 2: Preparing the chicken
- Cut breasts and thighs into 1-inch thick pieces
- Put cut pieces of chicken into a bowl
- Thoroughly mix marinade with the chicken
- Let chicken rest for at least 2 hours, to overnight

Step 3: Baking the chicken
- Preheat oven to 400 degrees F
- Put a sheet of foil on a baking sheet large enough to fit all the chicken without overcrowding too much
- Put all of the chicken on the baking sheet, be careful not to put too much marinade with the chicken
- Let cook for about however many minutes until done but not overcooked to hell

Step 4: Finishing off the chicken
- Remove the chicken from the oven and allow it to rest for a bit
- Slice the chicken into smaller shawarma-esque pieces
- Put half of the chicken into a pan (since the pan likely won't fit all of the chicken) on medium-high with 1tbsp of olive oil, and let go for a couple minutes until the really small pieces start getting nice and crispy
- Repeat for the other half

II. THE SALAD
Step 1: Preparing the veggies
- Finely chop 1/2 head of parsley
- Roughly dice 3 salad tomatos
- Cut 1/2 inch cross sections of baby gem lettuce, ditch the gross bottom

Step 2: Preparing the dressing
- Mix:
- some olive oil
- a little less white wine vineager
- 1/2 lemon, squeezed
- salt and pepper to taste

Step 3: Duh
- Get the salad dressed, and ready for school

III. THE SIDES
- Hummus
- Toum
- Whichever pickled stuff you chose
